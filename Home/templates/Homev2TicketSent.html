Who we are

Iris Documents provides reliable authentication of business-client interactions involving contracts, agreements, and important documents. All agreements and signatures are recognized as valid under the United States Electronic Signatures in Global and National Commerce (E-SIGN) Act. All records are kept secure and every Iris Document comes with an explicit audit of all changes made to that document.

What we do

Iris Documents helps to streamline your workflow by automating the agreements and contracts your business is involved in. Our customizable templates allow your business to complete identical transactions quickly and with ease. Simply create a template and mark where each client should sign. Select the template when creating a new document, and a pre-marked box will be given for each client where only the client's name and email needs to be entered.
